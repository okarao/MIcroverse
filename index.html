The Life of Pele

  • 1940: Pele was born on the 23rd of October 1940 in Tres Coracoes, Brazil.
  • 1956: Pele joined the Brazilian Soccer Team at Age 16.
  • 1958: Pele went to his first World Cup in Sweden.
  • 1962: Brazil won the World Cup.
  • 1966: Pele playing for Brazil lost the 1966 World Cup.
  • 1970: In the 1970 Mexico World Cup, Brazil won.
  • 1997: Pele became a Sports Minister.
  • 2000: Pele was named the best soccer player of the 1900 Century.
  • 2022: Pele, days before new year, dies at the age of 82.
